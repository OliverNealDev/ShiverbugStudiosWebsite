The boring but important page

Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect almost nothing, we never sell anything, and there are no cookies. Here's the detail.

Who We Are

Shiverbug Studios Ltd, an independent game development studio based in North East England, UK. You can reach us any time at contact@shiverbugstudios.com.

What We Collect, and Why

The contact form. If you use it, we receive the name, email address and message you type. It's delivered to us by Formspree, who process it on our behalf. We use it to reply to you, and for nothing else.

The newsletter. If you sign up, your email address is held by Buttondown, who send our emails for us. You'll confirm your address before you're subscribed, every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing removes you from the list.

Hosting. The site is hosted by GitHub Pages, which, like almost all web hosting, keeps standard server logs (such as IP addresses) for security. We don't see or use those.

What We Don't Do

No cookies. No analytics trackers. No advertising. No selling, renting, or sharing your details with anyone beyond the services named above. If that ever changes, this page will say so before it does.

How Long We Keep Things

Contact messages: as long as we need to deal with your enquiry, then they live in our inbox like everyone else's email. Newsletter addresses: until you unsubscribe.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law you can ask us what we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email contact@shiverbugstudios.com and we'll sort it. If you're unhappy with how we've handled something, you can complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version appears here. Last updated: 21 July 2026.
